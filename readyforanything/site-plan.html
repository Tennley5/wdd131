Overview

Purpose

This site will help users build personalized emergency kits based on specific scenarios like wildfires, earthquakes, or power outages. It will provide a simple list of things needed to build an effective kit.

Audience

The site is intended for individuals, families, and beginners who want to be more emergency-prepared

Dynamic elements

JavaScript will allow users to select an emergency scenario (wildfire, flood, blackout, etc.) and generate a checklist of supplies specific to that situation using arrays and filters. The checklist will appear dynamically on the page and users can: Add or remove custom items from their kit, Check off items as completed, Save their kit using localStorage, Print or download the final checklist. JavaScript will include functions, event listeners, conditional logic, and DOM updates to provide a responsive and helpful user experience.

Content

Home page

Be prepared for the unexpected. Whether it’s a wildfire, power outage, flood, or earthquake, having a ready-to-go emergency kit can make all the difference. This site will help you build a custom checklist based on your needs and location. Don’t wait for an emergency to start preparing—click the button below to build your kit now. Some quick tips to remember: keep at least one gallon of water per person per day, don’t forget medications or medical supplies, include a flashlight with backup batteries, and check your kit every six months for expired items.

[Page 2]

Select the type of emergency you want to prepare for using the dropdown menu. Based on your choice, a checklist will be created with recommended items like water, non-perishable food, flashlights, hygiene supplies, and important documents. You can check off items as you go, add custom items that fit your needs, and save your kit for later using your browser. Once you're ready, you’ll have the option to print or download your list. Everything is designed to help you stay ready, stay safe, and respond with confidence.
